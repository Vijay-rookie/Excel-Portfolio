## Project Synopsis: E-commerce Dashboard in Excel 
This project involved the design and development of an interactive Excel dashboard to analyze and visualize key performance metrics for an e-commerce business across 
various product categories—Fashion, Home and Furniture, Electronics, and Auto & Accessories.  

## Key Features: 
• Month-wise sales & Profit analysis and comparison with respect to product and category.<br>
• Region-wise sales with respect to product and category.<br>
• Key metrics: Total sales, Total profit and Total Quantity.<br>
• Data visualizations including bar charts and Histogram.<br>

## Tools & Techniques Used: 
• Excel Pivot Tables. <br>
• Drop-down menus for interactivity.<br> 
• Excel functions (e.g., OFFSET AND COMBO BOX). <br>
• Dashboard design principles for clarity and usability.<br>

## Outcome: 
The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor performance by category, identify trends, and make informed business decisions. It serves as a scalable template 
that can be updated with new data regularly, making it a valuable tool for ongoing e-commerce analytics.
